- **Reading**. I mostly read fiction and have read hundreds of books. I [catalog](/reading) every book I read. I welcome any recommendations.
- **Computer programming**. I post most of my code to [GitHub](https://github.com/PappasBrent/). I also enjoy solving coding problems, and have solved [over a thousand LeetCode questions]({% post_url 2023-09-30-365-days-of-leetcode %}).
- **Running**. I began running in high school in order to make weight for wrestling matches. Although I stopped wrestling after my sophomore year of high school, I continued to run. I prefer rigorous long runs. My fastest 7 miles time is 49 minutes, 33 seconds.
- **Game development**. I sometimes make video games with my friends and girlfriend. All my games are available on [Itch](https://brent-pappas.itch.io/).
- **Juggling**. After learning how to ride a unicycle in the 9th grade, I taught myself how to juggle in order to put the two acts together, and I soon became a little obsessed with juggling (in school I would isolate myself during lunch hour in order to practice juggling)! While I don't juggle as often anymore, I still occasionally practice, and hope to one day be able to juggle five balls. If you see me in person, feel free to ask me to juggle :)
- **Board games**. I always look forward to evenings playing board games with friends. I usually enjoy board games of mild complexity (think [Mahjong](/mahjong.md)).
