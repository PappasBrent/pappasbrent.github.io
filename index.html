I am a graduate student at the University of Central Florida (UCF) pursuing a PhD in Computer Science. My advisor is Dr. Paul Gazzillo.

I currently work as a graduate research assistant at UCF. I am currently focusing my research efforts on designing a means to convert C preprocessor usage to C language constructs. My research is related to Checked C, Microsoft Research's dialect of C that ensures spatial memory safety; and 3C, Correct Computation Inc.'s' tool for semi-automatic conversion from regular C code to Checked C code.
